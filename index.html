About Our College & IUCEE

The IUCEE Club at KLE Technological University is a student-driven initiative that unites inquisitive individuals who are passionate about engineering education, innovation, and collaboration. We collaborate closely with IUCEE (Indo Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education) to host workshops, mentoring programs, technical presentations, and community projects that facilitate student growth beyond traditional learning.

Our objective? To cultivate a robust community in which students support one another in becoming better engineers, leaders, and learners — all while enjoying the journey.
