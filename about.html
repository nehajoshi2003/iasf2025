About Us

IUCEE (Indo Universal Collaboration for Engineering Education) Club is a vibrant community of engineering students and mentors working together to bring innovation into classrooms, solve real-world challenges, and build meaningful global connections. We focus on leadership, sustainability, and empowering students to make a difference.
