About Us

The IUCEE Club at KLE Technological University is a student-led group that works to make engineering education more engaging, collaborative, and practical. We’re part of the IUCEE network, which connects students and educators across India and beyond to share ideas and improve how engineering is taught and learned.

What We Do

  • Workshops & Talks to explore new tech and skills
  • Mentorship for academic and personal growth
  • Community Outreach through service and innovation
  • National Events like IASF, where we proudly represent KLE Tech


Why Join?
It’s a space to grow, build meaningful connections, and be part of a community that supports learning, leadership, and real-world impact.

Our Mission

At the IUCEE Club of KLE Technological University, our mission is to create a space where students can grow not just as engineers, but as thoughtful and capable individuals. We aim to promote collaborative learning, critical thinking, and innovation by connecting students with mentors, experts, and real-world experiences. Through workshops, outreach programs, and national-level events, we strive to build a community that values curiosity, leadership, and social impact — preparing our members to take on challenges that go beyond the classroom.

Our Vision

Our vision is to build a vibrant student community at KLE Technological University where knowledge is shared freely, leadership is nurtured, and learning is driven by purpose. We aim to be a platform that inspires students to explore new ideas, work on meaningful projects, and contribute positively to society. By staying connected with global educational trends and embracing collaboration, we hope to shape future engineers who are not just skilled, but also socially responsible and globally aware.
